Stockfish 18
============

Kiebitz includes Stockfish, a free UCI chess engine developed by the Stockfish
authors and contributors. Stockfish is distributed under the GNU General Public
License version 3 (GPL-3.0). The complete license is included next to this file
as COPYING.txt.
